About Our Company

Our Story

Founded in 2006, AVEKS (Farkom Stab LLC) has grown to become a leading provider of computer technology in Uzbekistan. With our headquarters located in the Abu Saxiy market in Tashkent, we operate through 5 specialized stores offering a wide range of quality tech products.

What We Do

We specialize in retail and wholesale of computers and components, including:

  • Computers & All-in-Ones
  • Processors & RAM
  • HDD/SSD Storage
  • Graphics Cards
  • Interactive Whiteboards
  • Printers & Accessories

We source our products directly from manufacturers in China and the United Arab Emirates (Dubai), ensuring quality and competitive pricing.

Our Growth

With annual sales of 10-15 billion UZS, we continue to expand our market share and customer base each year. Our reputation for quality and service keeps customers coming back.

Our Team

Led by Director Faxriddin Atabayev, our team of 15 qualified professionals provides expert advice and top-notch customer service across all 5 locations.

Our Future

We plan to expand our retail network, develop online sales, broaden our product range (especially interactive products), and introduce new customer service options.

Why Choose AVEKS?

  • Comprehensive warranty and customer service
  • Competitive pricing and experienced staff
  • Convenient locations in Abu Saxiy market
